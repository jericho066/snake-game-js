Rules

OBJECTIVE

Eat food to grow your snake and score points. Survive as long as you can — hitting a wall or your own body ends the game (unless you are invincible).

CONTROLS

  • Use the arrow keys to steer the snake.
  • During the Poison effect controls are inverted (left ↔ right, up ↔ down).

FOODS

  • Apple - +1 point (default). Eating apples increases counters that cause other foods to appear.
  • Banana - +3 points. Causes the snake to shrink by several segments (amount depends on board size).
  • Orange - +5 points. Grants invincibility for a short time (you can pass through the snake body without dying while active). Duration depends on board size.
  • Grapes - +10 points. Activates ×2 score (points from food are doubled) for a short time. Duration depends on board size.
  • Poison - no points. When eaten it poisons the snake (controls invert) for 5 seconds. Poison spawns periodically and disappears if not eaten after a short time.

GAME OVER

  • Game over occurs if the snake hits the wall or its body (unless invincible).
  • When the game ends your score is saved (submitted once to the server).
  • Use Reset to restart the current game, or Home to go back to menus.
